Recreational Trip to Chefchaouen

By Ouisnaf Khalid | April 20, 2025

On Sunday, April 20th, 2025, Apex Club organized a recreational trip to the city of Chefchaouen with the objective of offering students a refreshing break from academic life while strengthening communication and social bonds among participants. This initiative reflects the club’s commitment to student well-being, teamwork, and creating meaningful connections beyond the university setting. By bringing students together in a relaxed and friendly environment, the trip aimed to promote unity, cooperation, and a strong sense of belonging within the Apex Club community.

The journey began early in the morning from Martil, where participants gathered with enthusiasm and positive energy. Along the way, the group made a stop at Nakhla Dam, allowing everyone to enjoy breakfast together and capture memorable photos in a calm natural setting. Upon arrival in Chefchaouen, participants explored the city’s iconic blue streets, admired its unique architecture, and enjoyed free time for photography, shopping, and cultural discovery. The trip also included various group activities and games, which encouraged interaction, teamwork, and moments of laughter, further strengthening relationships among students.

The day concluded with a shared lunch, marking a highlight of unity and collective joy. As participants returned in the evening, the atmosphere was filled with happiness, appreciation, and lasting memories. This recreational trip proved to be more than just a visit to a beautiful city—it was a meaningful experience that reinforced the values of love, friendship, unity, and brotherhood, leaving a positive and lasting impact on all those who took part.
